Welcome to Sofkafe

Sofkafe is a cozy and stylish café located in the heart of the city. Our café is the perfect place to relax, catch up with friends, or enjoy some alone time while sipping on our delicious coffee.

At Sofkafe, we pride ourselves on our carefully selected coffee beans, which we roast in-house to ensure that each cup of coffee is fresh and delicious. We also offer a variety of desserts and mugs for sale, so you can take a piece of Sofkafe home with you.
